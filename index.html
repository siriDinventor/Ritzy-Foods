Cereal Biltz

Cereal Blitz is a nutritional mix of 4 grain and a fruit: Corn, Millet, Soyabeans, groundnut & Dates that delivers all the essential amino acids, minerals and vitamins needed in the body for proper development, growth and repair as well as to combat malnutrition, stunting and wasting in a matter of weeks.
